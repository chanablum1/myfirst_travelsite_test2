Exploring Paris

I recently had the chance to visit Paris, the city of lights. It was an amazing experience exploring the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and strolling along the Seine river.

Paris truly lives up to its reputation as one of the most romantic cities in the world.

Here are some snapshots from my trip:

Go somewhere
ZooParc de Beauval

Zooparc Beauval is a really beautiful place, with such rare and exotic animals. All the complexes were clean and the viewing was perfect. The animals all looked happy and were very active. My family traveled from the UK and it was well worth the trip. The cable car is a brilliant way to access areas of the park and in itself a great experience that allows you to see everything from above. .

The old town of Nice

We crossed the old city many times, occasionally getting lost in the dozens of narrow streets. So much to see, including museums, cafes, town halls, music venues and more. It was the first sunny day for a while, so lots of people walking around but never seemed that crowded. Could spend hours here.. and we did.
